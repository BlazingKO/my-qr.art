my-qr.art

What is it?

With My-QR.art you can make QR codes that look like you want them to look. My-QR.art uses a unique approach to allow you to change up to 80% of your QR code, when other solutions on the web only allow up to 30%. Still you can let your QR code go to any URL you want! By giving your QR code a recognizable design, for example utilizing your logo, you can improve engagement, make your add stand out and increase the chance that your QR code is scanned.

How does it work?

Open the QR designer by pressing the big blue Design your QR button on the top of this page. There you can draw how you want the QR code to look, or simply upload any black and white image to set as the design. When you are done with your design, type in the URL for your QR code on the top of the page and press Create QR. The QR code will be generated for you in just a second and will work immediately!

But how is this possible?

If you want to know the nitty-gritty technical details, basically, instead of simply creating a QR code from your URL, we create data that fits your design and redirects to this domain. Then we can redirect that exact URL to your URL. This way the QR has your design but still redirects wherever you want. Technically this is not without it's challenges, QR codes are quite complex! If you want to know more, check out the code on GitHub.

Is it free?

Yes! My-QR.art is completely free to use and has no advertisements! You don't need to sign up or create an account. Simply create a QR code with the editor and share it with whomever you want! However, it costs money to host this site, so sadly at this point I can't guarantee your QR codes will work forever.

My-QR.art is free and open source software! Report bugs or contribute at the GitHub page.
