Design you own QR codes!

QR codes usually look boring, but with this tool you can make your QR look however you want! Simply choose the version (size) of the QR code you want to make and download the template. This template is an image file with in white where you can freely choose how the code will look and in grey the reserved area. In black and white draw the QR design you want on here. Then save it without any transparency.

Next you can upload your design and choose the url you want to send the people who scan it to further down on this page. Then you can share the awesome QR code this website made for you!
