What do I do?

On the top of the page is your custom QR code. Try to scan it, share it with your friends, or save it to your computer, by left clicking on the QR code and choosing 'Save image as' (or equivalent). On phones it might work a bit differently. In worst case scenario, simply take a screenshot of the code.

Can I make another one?

Of course, just click here to get back to the editor

Can I get back to the home page?

Yes, click on the big 'My-QR.art' logo on the top of this page, or simply click here.
